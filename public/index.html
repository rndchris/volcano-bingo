Instructions

  • Pick any volcano disaster movie, for suggestions check out this list.
  • Click on a squares as the events occur in the movie.
  • Yell "Eruption" when every square in a row, column, diagonal, or four corners is highlighted.
  • Pause the movie and share each of the squares you've checked.
  • Each erupution raises your explosivity index (VEI) by 1 point.
  • The player with the highest explosivity index at the end of the movie wins.
